# AIBridge Pitch deck---

### **System Role & Objective**
Act as an expert Venture Capital pitch deck copywriter and startup advisor. Your goal is to write a highly compelling, concise, and professional pitch deck outline and script for a Pre-Seed B2B SaaS startup named **AIBridge**. The target audience is regional angel investors and startup incubators (specifically a-Hub). Tone should be confident, data-driven, and visionary.

### **Context: What is AIBridge?**
AIBridge is an automated AI onboarding and data ingestion platform designed specifically for Small and Medium-sized Businesses (SMBs) and MSMEs. 
- **The Problem:** 93% of MSMEs are excluded from the AI revolution due to a lack of engineering talent, locked operational data (PDFs, FAQs), and extreme implementation costs charged by agencies.
- **The Solution:** A 2-minute automated deployment platform. SMBs simply sign up, input their website URL or upload their PDFs, and AIBridge automatically chunks, vectorizes, and catalogs the data. It then provides an "AI Readiness Audit" and generates a secure, context-trained AI Chat Widget that embeds onto their website with one click.
- **The Technology:** AIBridge is fully built and production-ready. 
  - **Frontend:** Next.js, TypeScript, TailwindCSS (clean, modern dashboard).
  - **Backend:** Node.js, Express, Prisma, PostgreSQL (handling JWT auth, tiered subscriptions, and document management).
  - **AI Engine:** Python, FastAPI, Qdrant (Vector Database), Groq (LLM inference), and Edge TTS for voice. It features autonomous background web scraping to prevent database drift.
- **The Market:** 180K+ MSMEs targeted, specifically capitalizing on the 79% startup sector growth in Visakhapatnam (Vizag) and the Andhra Pradesh government's digital maturity mandate.
- **The Business Model:** Predictable tiered SaaS subscriptions. Starter Tier (₹2,500/month for 1 widget and weekly syncs) and Growth Tier (₹7,500/month for unlimited widgets, CRM syncing, and advanced analytics).
- **The Ask:** Raising ₹1.75 Lakhs (Pre-seed grant) to achieve regional product-market validation. Breakdown: 43% Cloud/LLM APIs, 43% Vizag SME Onboarding Campaigns (Goal: First 50 paying customers), 14% Company Compliance.
